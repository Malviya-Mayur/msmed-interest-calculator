This tool calculates interest payable to MSME vendors whose invoices were settled beyond the credit period, as mandated by Section 16 of the MSMED Act, 2006. The statutory credit period is 45 days but you can adjust it below based on your case.

Enter the annual interest rate to apply. Per MSMED Act Section 16, the rate is typically 3× the RBI bank rate (e.g. RBI rate 6.5% → enter 19.5%).
